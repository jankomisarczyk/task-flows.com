Who am I?

I thought that the best way to answer 'Who am I?' question is to explain my sense of purpose, which is Japanese conecpt widely know under a term IKIGAI. It is more than your reason for living but also a philosophy to live a life full of happiness and enjoyment. It shows you your specific activites and dreams that are importontant to you and that makes you want to get up each day to turn them into rality. It is a combination of four elements: 1) what I love I found that I enjoy exercising, expecially doing calisthenics and gymnastics (be carefull not to break neck ;)). Apart from that I find peace of mind while playing piano and reading about philosophy or econmics. 2) what I am good at I have never had any problems with learning mathemathics and physics. These were alwayas my favourite subjects at school and I still enjoy solving math riddles or exploring beutiful and vast universe. 3) what I can be paid for The ways through which I currently make a living is being a Software Developer professionally and a Quant after hours. I find enjoyment from both coding and doing research about misspriced stocks (value and event driven investing). 4) what the world needs I belive that being a good person and living without violating my moral backbone is what the world needs. Apart from that I believe that in the near future we will see powerful Artificial General Inteligence and I want to be a part of the team that will make it happen.
